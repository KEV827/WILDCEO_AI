# WildCEO — Umbrel Community App Store

> *"I don't give advice. I give war room strategy."*

A community app store for [Umbrel](https://umbrel.com) containing the **WildCEO AI** strategic advisor.

## Install on your Umbrel

1. Open your Umbrel dashboard
2. Go to **App Store** → click **⋯** (top-right) → **Community App Stores**
3. Paste: `https://github.com/wildceo/wildceo-umbrel-store`
4. Click **Add** → **Open** → **Install WildCEO AI**

## What is WildCEO AI?

A self-hosted AI strategic advisor built on the [WildCEO manifesto](https://wildceo.live). Not a chatbot — a war room.

**Bring Your Own Key:** Anthropic (Claude) · OpenAI (GPT) · OpenRouter (200+ models) · Ollama (fully offline)

**Four War Room Modes:**
- ♟ **Strategy** — Asymmetric thinking for hard problems
- ⚔ **Fight** — Legal, corporate, or competitive warfare
- ⚡ **Build** — Startup, product, or team decisions
- 🔥 **Lead** — People, family, values, culture

Your keys. Your data. Your server. Always.
